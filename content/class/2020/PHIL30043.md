**<span class="caps">PHIL30043</span>: The Power and Limits of Logic** is a [University of Melbourne undergraduate subject](https://handbook.unimelb.edu.au/view/2020/PHIL30043). It covers the metatheory of classical first order predicate logic, beginning at the *Soundness* and *Completeness* Theorems, *Compactness*, *Cantor&rsquo;s Theorem*, the *Downward L&ouml;wenheim&ndash;Skolem Theorem*, *Recursive Functions*, *Register Machines*, *Representability*, the *Indefinability of Truth* and the *Undecidability of Predicate Logic*, and ending up at *G&ouml;del&rsquo;s Incompleteness Theorems* and *L&ouml;b&rsquo;s Theorem*.
