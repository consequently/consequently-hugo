Time flies when...

November 15, 2005

… you’re on sabbatical.

I’m now in St. Andrews visiting Arché for what can only be described as “November Madness.” I’m giving a seminar this afternoon in Arché/Philosophy (in the wonderful Edgecliffe building) and then tomorrow, I’m giving a seminar in computer science tomorrow. This means I’ll miss the Vagueness Seminar and a Philosophy Departmental Colloquium. However, I will be at the Revenge Workshop on Thursday, and the Vagueness Workshop for Friday and Saturday.

That should be enough to keep me going.
