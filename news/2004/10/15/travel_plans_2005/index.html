Travel Plans 2005

October 15, 2004

In the second half of 2005 we’re planning to travel, on sabbatical. The C, G and Z show will be based in Oxford for Michaelmas.

Before arriving in Oxford, we’ll be doing some European touring. C will be visiting a colleague in Denmark. I’ll hopefully be presenting at Logic Colloquium 2005 in Athens (July 28 to August 3), and ESSLLI2005 in Edinburgh (I should be on in the second week, August 15 to 19).
